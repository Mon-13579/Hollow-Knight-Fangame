There are going to be around 5 Shells in the full game.

1. Steel Shell.
Steel Shell is a very tanky Shell, it lowers your movement speed a bit, and increases damage slightly,
but it also lets you press the [Special] button to hide in your shell for ~3 seconds where you can move
at half speed, it primarily has Defensive item slots, with two Utilities, 1 Offensive, and 3 Defensive.
It also removes ALL double damage.

2. Voided Shell.
The Voided Shell is a very volitile Shell. Bathed in Void from the deep Abyss, this Shell is a gift
from ShadeCarer Pawe upon finishing their final quest. This Shell makes you immune to all
Void Damage, but removes one of your masks while you have it equiped.

3. Duelist Shell
The Duelist Shell is a dificult shell to master, with its low attack speed but high damage per hit,
you need to time hits well, or you'll get punished.
This Shell's gimick is Parrys, while you can Parry with any Shell, parrying with the Duelist Shell will
A) increase damage by x0.5 on your next hit, and
B) deal x0.5 of your damage to the thing you parried (when it would normally cancel out the damage on
both sides)
