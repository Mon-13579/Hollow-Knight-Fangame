ExoSkeletons

Exoskeletons are the equivilant of Crests in Crysalost

There are going to be around 5 ExoSkeletons in the full game.
1. Steel Shell.
Steel Shell is a very tanky crest, it lowers your movement speed a bit, and increases damage slightly,
but it also lets you press the [Special] button to hide in your shell for ~3 seconds where you can move
at half speed, it primarily has Defensive item slots, with two Utilities, 1 Offensive, and 3 defensive.
It also removes ALL double damage.
