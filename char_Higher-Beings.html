Higher Beings are the gods of the Hollow Knight world.
They can create whole biomes with a mere dream,
They can plague the dreams of every bug in a kingdom,
They can be the cause of a potentially live saving substance.

And the most elite among them
Pale Beings
Can give sentience to lesser bugs akin to beasts.

One of our Higher Beings is Kepril, the Higher Being of Rebirth.
This Higher Being is worshipped for being unkillable, and it is believed
that through worship of Kepril, one can achieve immortality, or something similar
