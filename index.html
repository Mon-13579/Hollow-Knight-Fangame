Hollow Knight: Crysalost is a basic fangame of Hollow Knight by Team Cherry. It follows the character Scarab, a stag beetle who weilds two halfs of a par of scissors.
Brave the Kingdom of Crysavale, meet many characters, collect items, earn ExoSkeletons, and end it all off, with taking down a monarch long lost to a plague.
