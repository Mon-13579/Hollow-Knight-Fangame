Items are seperated into 3 diferent types, Offensive (red), Defensive (Silver),
and Utility (Yellow)

Each type has a diferent function, Offensive (red) items are used offensively, being items like the Fragile Mask, which boosts damage a
bit upon being broken.
Defensive (Silver) tools are, as the name suggests, Defensive, being ones like the Thickened Mask, which gives one temporary mask.
Utility (Yellow) tools are passive effects that don't have much baring on combat, such as the Compass.
